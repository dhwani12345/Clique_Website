INTERESTSHIP

A project development program to flourish your interests!

Clique inaugurated its journey by Interestship 1.0 program. Despite being the foremost event, Clique got full community support and marked the presence 40 aspiring women in STEM. The community consisted of women working in different parts of the globe, having varied curriculum and years of expertise in given domain as well as working upon diversified technologies.

Timeline of INTERESTSHIP 1.0

The event lasted for 3 weeks starting from 7th June, 2020. Over the course of the event, the community was divided into teams working upon distinct captivating project titles but united by the end goal to fulfill the outcome for the project assigned. There were in total 8 project definitions and each team was allocated with mentors in the respective domains to provide the appropriate guidance.

During the stretch of the program, the community was not only aimed to have rightful coding and implementation skills for building the respective projects but was also focused upon presentation as well as writing skills. Clique has created its own publication for the community to help community emerge and express their work and ideas in writing.
