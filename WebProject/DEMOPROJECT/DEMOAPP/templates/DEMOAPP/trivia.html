TRIVIA EVENINGS

A fun way to increase your knowledge!

Ken Blanchard, a management expert and author of the famous book “The One Minute Manager” quotes – “None of us is as smart as all of us”, simple words with a lot of weight.

Most companies these days foster team building activities as teamwork has been a vital tool as far as employee-focused initiatives are concerned and its value in bringing about workplace happiness has remained untenable. Team building raises student morale and builds enthusiasm. Such fun activity helps our community bond, facilitates collaborative and motivated work culture, fosters responsive and meaningful communication and shares the knowledge which indeed helps us work, co-work and network together.

Trivia Eve 1.0

In one of our Team building activities during Interestship 1.0, we had a chance to play a Trivia game- an interactive fun quiz game. The game was organized online by means of Google Meet for the ease of participation of students and mentors all over the World. With a set of questions based on movies, logos, general knowledge, technology, series and much more, depicted with a pictorial representation, the participants actively decoded the image in search for an answer within a quick span of time. The questions were carefully designed to impart knowledge along with the fun. One of our quick witted community members “Swasti Khuranna” was the winner of the game.

Future Trivia Evenings

Clique has curated list of questions and also have done successfull online trivia evenings with 30+ participants. We're master at hosting fun events where we get people together for insightful trivial evenings so that they can learn together in a unique way. We also collaborate with different organizations to host such activity.
